About Us

SEAWAVE FORWARDING AND LOGISTICS PVT LTD have the ability to meet the everyday changes in the industry must be attributed to its dedicated and experienced staff.

SEAWAVE is more than just another freight forwarding company. It is a dynamic organization who meets the challenges of the ever-changing freight industry.

The company's efficient staff takes charge of the entire process of planning, implementing and controlling the efficient and cost effective flow of goods and related information from the point of origin to final destination, for the purpose of confirming to customer requirements.
The management team of SEAWAVE is made up of highly experienced freight forwarding professionals who share SEAWAVE's commitment to achieving the highest standards of excellence.

A Commitment to Quality :

A commitment to find the best solution for the individual needs of each customer, or as we like to think, each business partner. Studies are carried out for costs of transportation, for alternatives, customer service needs, communication etc.

In-house training programs are conducted on various subjects like Customs, Carrier Procedures and customer service.

Special Expertise

Our wealth of expertise includes understanding the unique challenges which our clients face.

We believe in team work with committed performance to meet the needs of customers up to their satisfaction, having well experienced, qualified & trained staffs, supported by fully computerized documentation, latest communication & networking system.

We use our sector specific knowledge and understanding of company requirements to ensure clients achieve their objectives.

About Our Managing Director

Nalinikant Mishra - The Managing Director - SEAWAVE FORWARDING AND LOGISTICS PVT LTD is one of the most renowned names in the logistics industry today. With a B.E. (Civil) degree to his credit he started his career with Asia foundation & constructions limited (AFCONS) as a Site Engineer, where as he was exposed to make strategy, planning and implementation.

There after he switched to the role of an EXIM Consultant to develop strategic layouts and design regarding logistic and custom clearance with his learnings, experience and qualified mind.

In 1996, after adequate working experience, learning and implementation practice with several top notch companies, he started practicing as a CHA Agent with a temporary license and in 2004 he incorporated his own company.

Mr. N.K. Mishra, believes that he has the responsibility to return what he has gained from society in a better form. He is associated with Rotary International as well as is an active member of ACIC and so forth have connected his life’s work to build a fruitful and better environment for all the individuals of the society and Director of M/S-Indo-Latin American Chamber of Commerce for Mumbai zone.
