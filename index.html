About Us

KarunBharat Helping Hand Foundation is a non-profit organization committed to social welfare, with a specific focus on supporting families affected by cancer. Recognizing the financial, emotional, and social challenges that cancer brings, we have established a platform where individuals, corporations, and organizations can come together to make a positive impact. Our organization is founded on principles of compassion, empathy, and community solidarity.

Our Mission

KarunBharat Helping Hand Foundation is dedicated to supporting families affected by cancer by fostering a sense of community and providing comprehensive assistance. Our mission is to create a compassionate society where no one fights alone. Through the contributions of our members and partners, we aim to alleviate the burdens faced by cancer-stricken families and offer them the necessary support to navigate through challenging times.

Our Vision

Our vision is to build a society committed to social welfare, where individuals, companies, and organizations join hands to make a positive impact. We envision a community of 1000 members actively contributing towards the well-being of cancer-affected families. By offering financial aid, medical assistance, and emotional support, we strive to create an environment where every family battling cancer feels cared for, supported, and resilient.

Our Purpose

The purpose of KarunBharat Helping Hand Foundation is to be the answer to someone's prayers. We aim to address the multifaceted challenges faced by families affected by cancer. Our purpose includes providing emergency financial and medical aid, arranging suitable accommodation for underprivileged patients, offering financial support for medical treatments, and ensuring the overall well-being of cancer-affected individuals and their families. Through our initiatives, we aspire to make a meaningful difference in their lives.

  • How does SEAWAVE deals with Trade Challenges?

    Seawave solves the basic challenges for trade in import and export by getting a vendor who can meet the technical & logistics aspects also complying for import and export, design complete logistics and set SOP for execution of work as per the customers requirement.

  • How does SEAWAVE handle a specific case?

    A client of ours, M/s Everest Kanto Cylinder Ltd., had to establish a plant in Gujarat in 2008. As the plant was being imported from the USA, Seawave provided all logistical support, managed cost analyses relating to custom provisions, and tagged the material to enable it to be identified. Teamwork and proper management allowed Seawave to achieve its objectives. The plant was successfully relocated from the United States to Gandhi Dham Gujarat, India.
